Data Deletion Request

You have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated personal data from SiteTrack.

Your privacy matters to us

We are committed to protecting your personal data. Upon receiving your request, our team will review and process it within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.

What will be deleted
  • Your account profile and login credentials
  • Attendance check-in and check-out records
  • Biometric and fingerprint data linked to your account
  • Location and GPS check-in history
  • All personal information submitted during registration
  • Any associated activity logs and reports
Please note

Data deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once processed, your account cannot be recovered. If you only want to take a break, consider contacting your site manager instead.
